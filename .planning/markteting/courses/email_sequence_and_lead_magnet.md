# Lead Magnet & Email Sequence Strategy

## Part 1: The Lead Magnet
**Objective:** Bridge the gap between "feeling inadequate" and "feeling capable" without overwhelming them.

**Title:** **"The AI Agent Starter Kit: 3 'Copy-Paste' Workflows to Automate Your First Task"**

**Format:** A simple PDF or Notion guide containing 3 specific prompts/workflows that turn ChatGPT into a pseudo-agent (e.g., "The Research Agent," "The Editing Agent," "The Planning Agent").

**Why this works:**
*   **Low Friction:** It's "copy-paste" (addresses fear of technical difficulty).
*   **Immediate Win:** They "build" something immediately.
*   **Positioning:** It frames them as a "builder" instantly, solving the identity crisis.
