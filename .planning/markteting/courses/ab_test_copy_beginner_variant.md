# A/B Test Copy Variants: Intro to AI Agents

Based on your **Beginner Avatar** (curiosity mixed with inadequacy) and the goal to shift them from "just ChatGPT" to "Agentic AI," here are two distinct copy variants to test.

## Strategy Overview

*   **Variant A (The "Relatable" Angle):** Focuses on the *emotional gap*—validating their feeling of falling behind and offering a safe bridge. It leans heavily on the **Identification** and **Hope** stages of your narrative arc.
*   **Variant B (The "Empowerment" Angle):** Focuses on the *capability gap*—highlighting the difference between "chatting" and "building." It leans on **Curiosity** and **Belief**.

---

## Variant A: "The Bridge" (Emotional/Safe)
*Hypothesis: Addressing the feeling of "inadequacy" directly will build trust and lower the barrier to entry.*

**Headline**
**You use ChatGPT every day. So why does it feel like everyone else is miles ahead?**

**Subheadline**
Stop just chatting with AI. Start building with it. A step-by-step path for non-coders to build real apps, automations, and agents—no technical background required.

**Hero CTA**
Start Building (Even If You Can't Code)

**Problem/Agitation**
You see the posts on X and LinkedIn. People building entire apps in minutes. Automating their whole workflow. Meanwhile, you're still just... asking questions and getting text answers. You know AI is powerful, but you don't know how to cross the bridge from "user" to "builder."

**The Solution (Agentic AI)**
The secret isn't learning to code. It's learning **Agents**. While ChatGPT waits for you to type, AI Agents *do work for you*. They are the missing link that turns your ideas into working software.

---

## Variant B: "The Upgrade" (Direct/Aspirational)
*Hypothesis: Focusing on the tangible "superpower" of Agents will trigger curiosity and desire.*

**Headline**
**Stop Chatting. Start Building.**

**Subheadline**
Most people use AI as a smart search engine. Learn the "Agentic" workflow that turns you into a software builder overnight—without writing a single line of code.

**Hero CTA**
Build Your First AI Agent

**Problem/Agitation**
ChatGPT is a tool. **Agentic AI is a workforce.** If you're only using AI to write emails or summarize text, you are leaving 90% of its power on the table. The gap between you and the "tech wizards" isn't code—it's knowing how to command agents.

**The Solution (Agentic AI)**
Imagine an AI that doesn't just talk back, but *executes*. It writes files, runs code, and browses the web to finish tasks. This course is your manual for the new engine of creation.

---

## Key Psychological Triggers Used

| Trigger | Variant A Application | Variant B Application |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Identification** | "Why does it feel like everyone else is ahead?" (Validates insecurity) | "Most people use AI as a smart search engine." (Creates "us vs them") |
| **Hope/Solution** | "A step-by-step path... no technical background." | "Turns you into a software builder overnight." |
| **Labeling** | Redefines them from "falling behind" to "future builder." | Redefines them from "chatter" to "commander." |
| **Simplicity** | Emphasizes safety: "Even if you can't code." | Emphasizes power: "Without writing a line of code." |
