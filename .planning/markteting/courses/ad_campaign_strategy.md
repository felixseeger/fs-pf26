# Ad Campaign & Marketing Strategy: Intro to AI Agents

## 1. Campaign Overview
**Objective:** Drive downloads of the "AI Agent Starter Kit" (Lead Magnet) $\to$ Upsell "Intro to AI Agents" Course.
**Primary KPI:** Cost Per Lead (CPL) < .00
**Secondary KPI:** ROAS on immediate upsell > 1.5

## 2. Target Audience (The "Beginner")
*   **Demographics:** Knowledge workers, freelancers, non-technical founders.
*   **Psychographics:** Feels "left behind" by the AI wave. Uses ChatGPT daily but feels limited. Wants to build but fears code.
*   **Platform Focus:**
    *   **Meta (FB/IG):** High volume, visual storytelling, great for "pattern interrupt" ads.
    *   **YouTube:** High intent, allows for longer "demo" style ads to prove simplicity.

## 3. Ad Angles & Hooks

### Angle A: The "Chat Trap" (Problem-Focused)
*   **Hook:** "Stop treating AI like a smarter Google."
*   **Core Message:** Chatting is passive. Agents are active. If you're just chatting, you're missing 90% of the value.
*   **Visual:** Split screen. Left: User typing endlessly. Right: User sipping coffee while Agent works.

### Angle B: The "Magic Trick" (Solution-Focused)
*   **Hook:** "Watch me build an app in 60 seconds without writing code."
*   **Core Message:** The barrier to entry isn't coding anymore. It's knowing how to command agents.
*   **Visual:** Screen recording of the "Text-to-App" workflow (The video concept we are generating).

### Angle C: The "Identity Shift" (Emotional)
*   **Hook:** "You are not a 'user' anymore. You are a 'builder'."
*   **Core Message:** Bridge the gap between feeling inadequate and feeling powerful.
*   **Visual:** Cinematic shot of a person overwhelmed by code vs. empowered by a simple agent interface.

## 4. Creative Concepts (To Be Generated)

### Concept 1: "The Construction" (Video)
*   **Format:** Short-form video (Reels/TikTok style).
*   **Visual:** A user types a simple command. The text lifts off the screen and self-assembles into a 3D digital structure (representing an app/agent).
*   **Audio:** Sound of mechanical assembly + satisfying "click" of completion.
*   **Text Overlay:** "Don't just chat. Build."

### Concept 2: "The Evolution" (Static Image)
*   **Format:** Infographic / Comparison.
*   **Visual:** Evolution chart. Monkey (Google) $\to$ Human (ChatGPT) $\to$ Cyborg/Superhuman (Agentic AI).
*   **Headline:** "The Next Step in Evolution."

## 5. Budget & Testing Structure
*   **Phase 1 (Validation):** 50/day. Test 3 Creative Concepts x 2 Headlines.
*   **Phase 2 (Optimization):** Kill losers after 3x CPL spend. Scale winners by 20% every 3 days.
*   **Retargeting:** Show "Social Proof" ads (testimonials) to anyone who visited the LP but didn't download.
